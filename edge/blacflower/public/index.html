WHAT IS BLACKFLOWER?

A highly configurable unidirectional networking platform. Blackflower serves as the ground for realtime distributed systems, which can be easily specified, rapidly developed and dynamically deployed. Any service provider can therefore react quickly to market and macro-environment events, resulting in a higher level of flexibility when taking business decisions, and greater competitive advantage.
