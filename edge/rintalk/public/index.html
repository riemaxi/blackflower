ABOUT US

RinTalk is our telephony app running on major mobile operatioln systems like IOS, Android and Raspberry PI OS. It allows users to select storage service providers in order to store contacts and personal settings.

As part of Blackflower ecosystem, RinTalk comes with the possibility to serve as witness, transporter as well as to sell activity related data.

But RinTalk is not only about texting and video chats, the users can also carry on more advanced sessions like presentations and live canvas.
